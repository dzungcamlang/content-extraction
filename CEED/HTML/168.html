      Bollywood celebs: Then and now
      Shabana Raza, fondly known as Neha, debuted in Bollywood starring opposite Bobby Deol in the movie 'Kareeb'. The actress was named Neha by the director of the film Vidhu Vinod Chopra after her character in 'Kareeb'. She went on to do films like 'Fiza', 'Hogi Pyaar Ki Jeet' and 'Rahul' to name a few. In the year 2006, she got married to one of Bollywood's ace actors Manoj Bajpai and the couple is blessed with a daughter named Ava Nayla. After the break, Neha returned with the movie 'Alibaug' in the year 2010. In an interview, she had revealed that she was forced to change her name to Neha, however she has returned to her original name 'Shabana'. She also revealed that she was happy being Mrs. Manoj Bajpai. The petite and pretty Shabana looks as beautiful and gorgeous as before...

      Bollywood celebs: Then and now

      TNN | Feburary 22, 2017
      1/97 This throwback picture of Shah Rukh Khan with his wife Gauri and son Aryan will drive away your midweek blues
      Theres not an ounce of doubt that Bollywoods Baadshah Shah Rukh Khan is the most charming man in tinsel town. While fans just cant get enough of romantic Raj, they also know what a doting father and a loving husband he is in real life.
      We recently stumbled upon this throwback picture of the actor with his wife Gauri Khan and elder son Aryan Khan.
      Shah Rukh and Gauri can be seen twinning in a grey sweatshirt and posing for an adorable photo with their firstborn. Aryan, the little munchkin is trying to pop his head and pose for the photo too. Isnt it absolutely cute?
      Theres a huge transformation in Aryan from being the round cutesy baby to the handsome hunk he is today. He is currently pursuing his higher studies and fans cant wait to watch him make his Big Bollywood entry.

      2/97This picture of Shah Rukh Khan is going viral
      Shah Rukh Khan has come a long way ever since the release of his blockbuster Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge which hit the theatres in 1995. It was this Aditya Chopra-directed film that catapulted SRK to the league of superstars. Not just that, the actor became the undisputed king of romance with the success of this film a distinction that he holds to date. More than two decades later, the father of three continues to rule Bollywood with a fan following in millions.

      This still of Shah Rukh from Ho Gaya Hai Tujhko To Pyaar Sajna has been doing the rounds online since Tuesday morning. One look at it and youre transported back to the old school romance of Raj and Simran.

      3/97Sonam Kapoors throwback picture is extremely cute
      Sonam Kapoors Instagram is filled with her posing in designer clothes, beautiful locations and all things pretty. After vacationing in the snowy valleys of Altaussee, the Neerja actress posted this throwback picture on social media.

      Clad in her school uniform, with a water-bottle at an arms length, Sonam paints the vintage picture with an innocent stare to the camera. She captioned the image, I love some random memories that make me forget the complications of being grown up.. #rainbowsandbutterflies #childhood

      We all want to forget the complications of grown-up life, dont we?

      4/97You dont want to miss this pre-wedding throwback picture of Mira Rajput trying on an uncut-diamond necklace
      Shahid Kapoor made headlines when he announced his marriage to a Delhi-based Mira Rajput. The lady had no connection to Bollywood whatsoever and everyone was curious to know more about her. No wonder, Shahid-Mira's wedding ceremonies were covered with much enthusiasm by the media and saw an overwhelming presence of Bollywood celebrities.
      Now proud parents to a gorgeous baby girl Misha, the two recently appeared on Karan Johars chat show, Koffee with Karan and their fans got a glimpse of the real Mira Rajput, for the very first time. But quite possibly, Shahids fans are still curious to know more about his lovely wife, and that explains why a throwback picture of Mira Rajput that recently surfaced on the net, has gone crazy viral.
      In the picture, Mira can be seen trying out some pieces of jewelry that she wore for her wedding. It's the same Navratna polki choker and a pair of earrings that she had teamed up with her bridal lehenga, a pale pink rose coloured Anamika Khanna creation.

      5/97Here's what 'Aashiq Banaya Aapne actress Tanushree Dutta looks like now!
      Tanushree Dutta shot to fame immediately after winning the Miss India title in 2003. She went on to compete in the Miss Universe pageant and landed up in the top 10. Post which, many film offers came the beauty queens way. Tanushree took the nation by storm with her sizzling performance in Aashiq Banaya Aapne with the then serial kisser Emraan Hashmi.

      She went on to do a few more forgettable films, and soon vanished from the film industry. Recently, Tanushrees sister Ishita Dutta, who acted in Drishyam shared a picture of her. The former Miss India Universe title holder looks very different in this photo, complete with geek glasses and a cute chubby face. Would you like to see Tanushree back on the big screen?
